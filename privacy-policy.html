    1. Introduction

    Welcome to our privacy policy. At nguyenbacson.io.vn, we are committed to protecting the privacy and personal information of all users. This policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and disclose your information when you visit our website. All user IP information will be automatically collected, analyzed, and strictly encrypted by our partners' AI (Google Analytics and Cloudflare Analytics).

    2. Information We Collect

    2.1. Automatically Collected Information

    • IP Address: We collect your IP address to analyze traffic, protect the website from cyber attacks, and improve user experience.
    • Browser Information: Browser type, version, language, and display settings.
    • Device Information: Device type, operating system, screen resolution.
    • Access Data: Pages you visit, access time, time spent on pages.

    2.2. Information from Third Parties

    We may receive information about you from analytics service providers such as Google Analytics and Cloudflare Analytics.

    3. Purpose of Information Collection

    We use collected information for the following purposes:

    • Website Security: Detect and prevent fraudulent activities, DDoS attacks, and unauthorized access.
    • Analysis and Improvement: Understand user behavior to optimize website experience.
    • Legal Compliance: Meet current legal requirements and regulations.
    • Technical Support: Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues.

    4. Legal Basis for Data Processing

    Our data processing is based on the following legal grounds:

    • Your Consent: Through the consent banner when accessing the website.
    • Legitimate Interests: To protect the website and users from security threats.
    • Legal Obligations: To comply with current laws and regulations.

    5. Information Sharing

    We DO NOT sell, rent, or exchange your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes. However, we may share information in the following cases:

    • Service Providers: With partners providing services like Cloudflare, Google Analytics to operate the website.
    • Legal Requests: When requested by competent government authorities.
    • Protecting Rights: To protect our and users' rights, property, or safety.

    6. Information Security

    We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your information:

    • Data transmission encryption using SSL/TLS
    • Limiting information access to authorized personnel only
    • Storing anonymized information when possible
    • Regular security measure evaluations

    7. Data Retention Period

    We only store your information for the necessary period to fulfill the stated purposes:

    • IP address data: 12 months
    • Access log data: 6 months
    • Anonymous analytics data: 24 months

    8. User Rights

    According to data protection regulations, you have the following rights:

    • Access Right: Request to view information we store about you.
    • Correction Right: Request correction of inaccurate information.
    • Deletion Right: Request deletion of your personal information.
    • Consent Withdrawal Right: Withdraw consent at any time.
    • Objection Right: Object to the processing of your data.

    9. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

    We use cookies and similar technologies to:

    • Maintain login sessions (if applicable)
    • Remember user preferences
    • Analyze traffic
    • Detect fraudulent activities

    10. Contact

    If you have any questions about this privacy policy or wish to exercise your rights, please contact:

    Email: bacson20062007@gmail.com

    Website: https://nguyenbacson.io.vn/

    Response Time: We commit to responding within 30 business days.

    11. Policy Changes

    We may periodically update this privacy policy. Any changes will be announced on this page with the latest update date. We recommend reviewing this policy regularly.

    12. Effectiveness

    This privacy policy is effective from October 18, 2025, and replaces all previous privacy policies.

    By continuing to use our website, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to this privacy policy.
